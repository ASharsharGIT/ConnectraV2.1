Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

How quickly can you respond to urgent support requests?

We provide 24/7 emergency support with response times of 30 minutes for critical issues and 2 hours for standard support requests during business hours.

Do you provide free consultations for new projects?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations to understand your requirements and propose the best solutions for your business needs.

What industries do you specialize in?

We serve Government, Financial Services, Healthcare, Education, Retail, Manufacturing, and many other sectors with industry-specific solutions and compliance expertise.

Can you work with our existing IT team?

Absolutely! We collaborate closely with internal IT teams, providing knowledge transfer, training, and ongoing support to ensure successful project outcomes.

What kind of ongoing support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive support including 24/7 monitoring, preventive maintenance, security updates, performance optimization, and help desk services.

How do you ensure project security and confidentiality?

We maintain ISO 27001 certification, use secure development practices, sign NDAs for all projects, and implement strict access controls throughout the engagement.
